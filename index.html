Cmu Research

In the summer of 2018, I was a research assistant at the Human-Computer Interaction Institute of Carnegie Mellon University, working with Dr. Brad Myers. I performed a usability evaluation of Apache Beam, cloud computing software developed primarily by Google. I presented to Google engineers about concerns over their mental model, and also created Alabaster, a PyCharm plugin designed to improve discoverability when using Beam. After the summer, I coauthored a paper that has been submitted to Chi Late-Breaking Work.
